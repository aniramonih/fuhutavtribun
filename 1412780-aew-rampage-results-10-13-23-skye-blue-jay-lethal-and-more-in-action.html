AEW Rampage Results (10/13/23): Skye Blue, Jay Lethal, and More In Action

August 2024 · 1 minute read

All Elite Wrestling delivered an action-packed show with the October 13 episode of AEW Rampage.

Tonight’s episode will see The Gates of Agony take on the Blackpool Combat Club in tag team action, and Skye Blue going one-on-one with Emi Sakura in singles action. Elsewhere in the night, a trios bout will take place with Daniel Garcia, Daddy Magic, and Cool Hand Ang taking on The Hardys and Brother Zay.

Jay Lethal will also take part in a match, when he faces off against Trent Beretta in a singles match.

After the match, Magic and Garcia had a brief tense moment that didn’t culiminate in anything, but might be worth watching going forward.
