All About Chelsey Jordan From Season 3 Of Love Is Blind?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Chelsey Jordan, a 27-year-old manager of customer success, is one such competitor who believes in pursuing her goals. Jordan is eager to participate in the Netflix dating experiment in the hopes of meeting “the one.”

The third season of Love is Blind is about to return with a new group of enthusiastic, love-hungry individuals. The program will send 15 men and 15 women on consecutive blind dates until they determine whether or not they have found true love.

The synopsis of the reality dating show on Tudum on Netflix is as follows:

“Prepare your gold goblets: Love Is Blind will return for its third season on October 19. The dating experiment’s hosts, Nick, and Vanessa Lachey return for the next episode, which will expose viewers to people eager to date — and even get engaged – eye unseen.”

The program will debut on the streaming giant at 3 a.m. ET on October 19.

Chelsey Jordon is searching for someone as quirky as herself The third season of Love Is Blind

The third season of Love is Blind will focus on Chelsey Jordan’s search for love. The 27-year-old customer success manager is from Dallas, as are most of the candidates this season.

She recently moved to Chicago with her pet puppy, Sunny, in March 2022. Jordan announced the birth of Sunny on Instagram with the caption:

“In honor of the vernal equinox, I am embarking on a new chapter in my life! Meet Sunny, sometimes referred to as Sunny Rabbit and Sunny with a Chance.”

Chelsey Jordan, a candidate for season 3 of Love Is Blind, is looking for someone who can make her laugh. Her Instagram account currently has 1,500 followers, but this number is anticipated to jump after the show airs.

It appears that Chelsey enjoys spending time outdoors with her friends. According to her Love is Blind biography, Jordan is an adaptable, outgoing woman willing to “rock out.” She enjoys learning about the culture and her plants and hopes to find someone who can “spark her imagination.”

Her greatest pet peeve is encountering individuals who are “unwilling to explore new things.” According to Jordan, such individuals are “very irritating.”

Nevertheless, her bio states:

“I believe in achieving my life goals, even if it means finding love in a box.”

Jessica Gumbert, Julian Torres, Kalekia Adams, Kimberlee Clarke, Loren Langenbeck, Matt Bolton, Nancy Rodriguez, Nash Beuhler, Raven Ross, Sikiru Alagbada, Simmer Bajwa, Tony Taylor, Valerie Truong, Zach Gordon, and Zanab Jaffrey will feature alongside Chelsey Jordan on Love is Blind season 3.

Others who have joined them include Alexa Alfia, Amanda Peterson, Andrew Liu, Anthony LaScalea, Ashley Randermann, Bartise Bowden, Brannigan Maxwell, Brennon Lemieux, Charita Scott, Cole Barnett, Colleen Reed, Dale Dalida, and DaVonte Black.

Additional information about the program

The unique sociological experiment aims to explore whether individuals may fall in love without ever meeting or if physical courtship is necessary. Vanessa and Nick Lackey, who has hosted the program since its inception, will host Love is Blind’s third season.

The Netflix series will have 12 episodes, which will be distributed in four parts. The first batch will air on October 19, followed by three episodes on October 26, and the subsequent batch on November 2.

The final set of episodes will be uploaded on the platform on November 9 at 3 a.m. EST. Don’t miss Chelsey Jordan and the other contestants on October 19 at 3 a.m. ET premiere of Love Is Blind season 3 on Netflix.
