Barry Van Dyke is an American actor best known for his role as Lieutenant Detective Steve Sloan on Diagnosis: Murder. In the show, Barry appeared alongside his father, Dick Van Dyke, who is an award-winning entertainer. Dick is globally known for appearing in the CBS sitcom The Dick Van Dyke Show.

Barry often works with his father appearing in several other television shows. Besides, Diagnosis: Murder, Barry's tv credits include Remington Steele, Magnum, P.I., The Redd Foxx Show, etc. Now, let's get to know some exciting details about Barry.

Barry Van Dyke Wife

The 69-year-old, Barry Van Dyke has been married to his longtime wife, Mary Carey Van Dyke since 1974. There is no additional information about their relationship history. However, the couple first met in 1967 as per sources.

Barry and his wife, Mary has been married for 46 years. To date, the pair haven't been involved in any controversies. Additionally, they share four children; three sons, Carey, Shane, and Wes along with a daughter named, Taryn.

Back in 2017, the Diagnosis: Murder actor became the victim of a death hoax. Further, several online tabloids wrote fake death news. As of 2021, Barry along with his wife and their children are happy, healthy, and successful.

Barry Van Dyke and his all four kids have worked with Dick Van Dyke!

Parents and Siblings

Barry Van Dyke was born on July 31, 1951, in Atlanta, Georgia, the United States. He is the second child of Dick Van Dyke and his ex-wife, Margie Willett. Barry has one older brother, Christian, and two younger sisters, Stacy and Carrie Beth.

While still married to Margie, Dick had a partner, Michelle Triola Marvin. They were in relation from 1976 until Michelle died in 2009. Besides, his first wife Margie passed away due to cancer in 2008. Afterward, Dick has been married to Arlene Silver since 2012.
