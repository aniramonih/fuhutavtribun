Brent Faiyaz is an American R&B singer who gained fame after being featured on GoldLink‘s single “Crew” alongside Shy Glizzy in 2016, which earned him a nomination for the Grammy Award for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration and was certified 6× Platinum by the RIAA.

In 2021, his singles “Wasting Time” featuring Drake and The Neptunes, “Gravity” with DJ Dahi featuring Tyler, the Creator, and “Mercedes” all entered the Billboard Hot 100. His second album, Wasteland, debuted at number two on the Billboard 200 chart.

Early Life
