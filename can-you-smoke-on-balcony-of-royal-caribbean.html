Can you smoke on the balcony of Royal Caribbean?

Yes, you can smoke on the balcony of Royal Caribbean ships. However, there are certain designated areas for smoking on the ships to ensure the comfort and safety of all passengers. It’s important to respect the rules and regulations regarding smoking on the balcony to avoid any inconvenience to other passengers and to maintain the cleanliness of the ship.

FAQs about smoking on the balcony of Royal Caribbean

1. What are the designated smoking areas on Royal Caribbean ships?

On Royal Caribbean ships, there are designated smoking areas such as the port side of the ship and the starboard side of the ship. These areas are designated to ensure that non-smoking passengers are not affected by the smoke, and to maintain the cleanliness of the ship.

2. Can I smoke on my balcony if I book a stateroom with a balcony?

Yes, you can smoke on your balcony if you have booked a stateroom with a balcony. However, it’s important to be considerate of other passengers and to ensure that the smoke does not affect them. It’s advisable to use an ashtray and to dispose of cigarette butts properly.

3. Are there any restrictions on smoking on the balcony?

There are certain restrictions on smoking on the balcony, such as not being allowed to smoke in indoor areas, near food venues, or in any area where there is a fire hazard. It’s important to adhere to these restrictions to ensure the safety and comfort of all passengers.

4. Can I bring my own cigarettes on board?

Yes, you can bring your own cigarettes on board. However, it’s important to check the customs regulations of the ports of call to ensure that you are not carrying more than the permitted amount of cigarettes. This will help you avoid any inconvenience during the cruise.

5. What is the smoking policy for electronic cigarettes and vaping devices?

Electronic cigarettes and vaping devices are subject to the same smoking policy as traditional cigarettes. Passengers are allowed to use electronic cigarettes and vaping devices in the designated smoking areas on the ship.

6. Are there any penalties for smoking in non-designated areas?

There are penalties for smoking in non-designated areas, as it is considered a violation of the ship’s smoking policy. Passengers who do not adhere to the smoking policy may be subject to fines or other consequences, so it’s important to respect the rules regarding smoking on the ship.

7. Can I smoke cigars on the balcony?

Cigars are generally not allowed in the designated smoking areas on Royal Caribbean ships. If you are a cigar smoker, it’s best to inquire with the ship’s staff about the specific policy regarding cigar smoking to ensure that you are not in violation of the rules.

8. How can I dispose of my cigarette butts on the balcony?

It’s important to dispose of cigarette butts properly to maintain the cleanliness of the ship. Most balconies are equipped with ashtrays for this purpose, and it’s important to use them to dispose of your cigarette butts responsibly.

9. Are there any special considerations for smoking on the balcony?

Passengers who smoke on the balcony should be considerate of their neighbors and ensure that the smoke does not affect other passengers. It’s important to be mindful of the wind direction and to avoid smoking in a way that may inconvenience other passengers.

10. Are there any restrictions on the type of cigarettes that can be smoked on the balcony?

There are no specific restrictions on the type of cigarettes that can be smoked on the balcony, as long as they are used in accordance with the ship’s smoking policy. It’s important to be mindful of the rules and regulations regarding smoking to ensure a pleasant experience for all passengers.

11. Can I smoke on the balcony of my stateroom if I have non-smoking neighbors?

If you have non-smoking neighbors, it’s important to be considerate of their preferences and to ensure that the smoke from your balcony does not affect them. It’s advisable to communicate with your neighbors and to find a mutually agreeable solution regarding smoking on the balcony.

12. Is there a time restriction for smoking on the balcony?

There are no specific time restrictions for smoking on the balcony, as long as it is done in accordance with the ship’s smoking policy. Passengers are free to smoke on their balcony at any time, provided that they are considerate of other passengers and adhere to the designated smoking areas.
