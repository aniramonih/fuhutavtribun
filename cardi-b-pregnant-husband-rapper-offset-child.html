Is rapper Cardi B pregnant again?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Cardi B (Source: Instagram)

Cardi B is all set to become a mother again! The rapper surprised everyone with her pregnancy news with her husband Offset during the Migos’ performance at the BET awards.

After her husband and the Migos finished their Straightening performance, she joined them for Type S–. Cardi made an appearance on the stage in a black bodysuit full of rhinestones and a sheer black mesh around the abdomen to showcase her pregnant belly.

Likewise, Cardi and Offset have a daughter Kulture Kiari together and they are having another child together. BET Awards made a tweet,

“OMG!!!!!! @iamcardib breaking some MAJOR news! Pregnant and slaying Star-struck#BETAwards #CulturesBiggestNight”

Cardi also a pregnancy photoshoot post on her Instagram where she painted white on her chest and belly. She captioned,

“#2!”

She and Offset married each other in September 2017 and have faced lots of troubles in their married life. They even filed for divorce last fall but got back together by November. Since then they have been enjoying each other’s company. The couple is yet to announce the gender, name, and due date of the coming baby.
