Carol Minogue is a former ballet dancer. 

She is best known as the mother of Kylie Ann Minogue, an Australian singer, songwriter, and actress.

Her Family and Children

She is happily married to Ron Minogue. Her husband is an accountant by profession. 

The couple exchanged the wedding vows when she was only 20 years old.

She and her husband are parents to three children, namely Kylie, Dannii and Brendan. 

Her daughters Kylie and Danielle, are single as of now. However, her son Brendan is a married man.

Dannii Paid Tribute to Her Mother’s Dream

Carol was previously a ballet dancer, and it was her dream to pursue her career as a dancer. 

However, she gave up on her dreams to take care of her family.

Her daughter Dannii paid tribute to her dream of becoming a ballet dancer in the premiere of Dance Boss. 

During the promotion of the show Dannii, she mentioned about finding her mother’s ballet shoes. 

She said, 

“I want to get her shoes framed in a beautiful box because dance is such a big part of who she was, and how she feels.”

Carol is Not Only a Mother, But also a Grandmother

Carol has two grandchildren, namely Ethan Edward Minogue Smith and Charles Minogue. 

Ethan is the son of Dannii Minogue and Kris Smith, an ex-professional rugby league player. 

Dannii and her partner started dating in August 2008. The couple met when Smith was celebrating his 30th birthday in Ibiza. 

The announcement of her pregnancy was made on January 9, 2010. The big revelation was made after receiving a 12-week scan.

She gave birth to her little kid on July 5, 2010. Two years after birth of her child, she split up with her partner. 

Charles is the son of Brendan and his wife, Rebecca.
