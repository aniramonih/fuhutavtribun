Redeem free operators, blueprints, double XP tokens, and more with these MW3 codes.

The much awaited Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 is here featuring brand new campaign, battle pass, rewards, and more. While players can unlock a lot of stuff for free by simply playing matches and completing challenges, a lot of items are locked behind the paywall. However, Activision runs various offers that can give codes to redeem and here are all the promotions available at the moment and rewards you will get from them.

All COD MW3 Redeem Codes Offers & Rewards

Activision announces multiple offers regularly; however, make sure to grab your rewards as soon as possible as some of them are available for a limited time only.

  • Nemesis Operator Pack Including Price, Ghost, Makarov & Warden Operators & Fate Weapons Vaults — Included in the Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III Vault Edition (No code or redemption required).
  • BlackCell Battle Pass Bundle — Included when pre-ordering the Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III Vault Edition (No code or redemption required).
  • Soap Operator Pack including the Masked Soap & Unmasked Soap Operator Skins and the Shadow Siege Weapon Blueprint — Included when pre-ordering any digital edition of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III (No code or redemption required).
  • Lockpick Operator Pack including the Lockpick, the Amrican Gothic SMG Blueprint, and the Starry Knife Blueprint — Included when pre-ordering any digital edition of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III through the PlayStation Store (No code or redemption required).
