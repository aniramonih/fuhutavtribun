About Denny Hamlin

James Dennis Alan Hamlin, born on November 18, 1980, is a successful American professional stock car racing driver and NASCAR team owner. He currently races full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series, driving the No. 11 Toyota Camry XSE for Joe Gibbs Racing.

In addition to his driving role, Hamlin co-owns and manages 23XI Racing, a NASCAR Cup Series team, with basketball Hall of Famer Michael Jordan. Hamlin has achieved multiple victories in his racing career, totaling 51 NASCAR Cup Series wins.

Among his notable triumphs are winning the Coca-Cola 600 in 2022 and the Daytona 500 in 2016, 2019, and 2020. He notably became the first driver since racing legends Richard Petty, Cale Yarborough, and Sterling Marlin to win consecutive Daytona 500s.
