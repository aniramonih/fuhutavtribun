His MLB Journey

DJ attended Glendora High School in his hometown. The Chicago Cubs drafted him in the 36th round of the 2014 Major League Baseball draft but did not sign. Instead, he attended Western Nevada College to play college baseball.


Peters was drafted by the Los Angeles Dodgers in the fourth round of the 2016 MLB draft. He signed for the team and began his professional career with the Ogden Raptors.

The 6-foot-6-inch played with the Rancho Cucamonga Quakes in 2017 and spent the 2018 season with the Tulsa Drillers.  Afterward, he was promoted to the AAA Oklahoma City Dodgers.


On 23 April 2021, he was promoted to the major leagues for the first time. Subsequently, he made his MLB debut that day against the San Diego Padres.

DJ was claimed off waivers by the Texas Rangers on 2nd August 2021.
