He Started His Career in the Late 1990s

Monaghan started his acting career in 1996, portraying the role of Geoffrey Shawcross on a crime drama series, Hetty Wainthropp Investigates.

But it was after he played the character of Charlie Pace in a drama series, Lost, which earned him widespread recognition. Likewise, he is also best known for portraying the character of Eminem in his hit song featuring Rihanna, Love The Way You Lie. 

Besides, the actor has appeared in popular movies and TV series such as 100 Code (2014), Pet (2016), and Atomica (2017).

In 2018, Monaghan portrayed the lead role of Senior Constable Stephen Langley on the Australian crime thriller television series, Bite Club. The series was broadcasted on the Nine Network starting from August 15, 2018.
