Don Orsillo Bio | Wiki

Don Orsillo is an American sportscaster working for the San Diego Padres as a play-by-play announcer on Bally Sports San Diego. Before joining Ball Sports in 2016, he was working for NESN as a play-by-play man for the Boston Red Sox.

Don Orsillo Age

He was born on December 16, 1968, in Melrose, Massachusetts, in the United States. Don is 54 years old.

Don Orsillo Education

He is a graduate of both John H. Fuller Elementary School in New Hampshire and also Kennett Junior High School in Conway, New Hampshire. Don later proceeded to Miraleste High School where he graduated in 1987 in Rancho Palos Verdes, CA.
