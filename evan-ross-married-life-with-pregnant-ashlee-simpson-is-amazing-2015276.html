Evan Ross: Married Life With Pregnant Ashlee Simpson Is &quot;Amazing&quot;

August 2024 · 2 minute read

And baby makes three! Evan Ross opened up to Us Weekly exclusively about life with his pregnant wife, Ashlee Simpson, and the two stars' plans for their baby on the way. 

The Hunger Games actor, 26, caught up with Us at the opening of STK Los Angeles at the W Los Angeles on Thursday, June 25. Ross has been busy promoting his new single "How To Live Alone" with T.I. but his schedule is just as packed back home as he and Simpson, 30, prepare for their first child together.

"We've got a baby on the way so there's gonna be a lot of surprises!" said Ross, who added that married life with Simpson is "amazing." "I'm just excited!"

The son of iconic singer Diana Ross, the ATL star said his "main goal" is to spoil his soon-to-be daughter. "I feel like she's gonna come into this world spoiled regardless between my family, my wife's family… they can't help it," he explained.
