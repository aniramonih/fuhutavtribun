Fans discover Steve Harvey recommended Amir Mathis to ex-wife

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Steve Harvey is renowned for his dating advice on his show, the Steve TV Show. But did you know that he played an unwitting matchmaker role by recommending Amir Mathis to his ex-wife? In this article, we’ll explore the surprising connection between Steve Harvey, Amir Mathis, and Linnea Hansson, along with the twists and turns in their relationship.

The Matchmaker Moment

In 2018, during a segment of the Hey Steve show, Linnea Hansson, Amir Mathis’ ex-wife, approached Steve Harvey with a dating dilemma. She sought advice on whether she should move in with her boyfriend, whom she had been dating for just four months. At the time, Linnea left Steve Harvey and the audience puzzled by not revealing her boyfriend’s identity but mentioning that he was a producer.

It was a moment of revelation when Steve Harvey realized that the mysterious producer Linnea was referring to was none other than Amir Mathis, the son of Greg Mathis, a dear friend of the TV host.

Meet Linnea Hansson

Linnea Hansson, Amir’s ex-wife, currently works as a community manager in Los Angeles, California. Originally from Helsingborg, Sweden, Linnea relocated to the United States when she started dating Amir. Her professional journey includes working with the UN Environment Programme, Rule Communication, and Kodiak Hub, where she held roles as a customer success manager.

Linnea and Amir tied the knot in June 2020, marking the culmination of their two-year-long relationship that began on a picturesque Swedish beach. Amir proposed to Linnea in her homeland, Tanzania, in 2019, adding a touch of romance to their love story.

Steve Harvey’s Unexpected Recommendation

During Linnea’s appearance on the Steve TV Show, she hesitated to reveal her boyfriend’s identity and workplace. However, Steve Harvey soon connected the dots and realized she was talking about his show’s producer, Amir Mathis. In response, Steve Harvey offered his enthusiastic endorsement of Amir:

“Sister, I personally vouch for this cat right here. This is actually one of my dear friend’s [Greg Mathis] sons. And you weren’t even going to tell me he worked here. I highly recommend this young man right here.”

Amir shared more about their relationship, including their meeting on a vacation in Sweden and his decision to extend his trip with a friend after their serendipitous encounter.

The End of a Marriage

Unfortunately, Amir and Linnea decided to part ways earlier this year. Their journey was not without its challenges, including Linnea’s travel to the United States for their 2020 wedding, which faced potential disruptions due to quarantine laws.

Gossip Next Door reported that the couple filed for divorce in Los Angeles in January 2022. On social media, Amir removed all pictures featuring Linnea, while she also made no mention of him on her profiles.

Amir has since introduced his new girlfriend, Sally, to his parents. Sally, originally from Korea, moved to Los Angeles at a young age. The couple is taking their relationship slow, navigating the complexities of building a new connection after a significant life change.

Conclusion: A Surprising Twist in a Love Story

The unexpected matchmaking role played by Steve Harvey in recommending Amir Mathis to his ex-wife, Linnea Hansson, adds a unique twist to their love story. While their marriage may have come to an end, both Amir and Linnea are moving forward with their lives, exploring new beginnings and forging new connections. The influence of unexpected connections and moments of revelation can leave a lasting impact on the journey of love and relationships
