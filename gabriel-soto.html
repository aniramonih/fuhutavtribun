Gabriel Soto Bio | Wiki

Gabriel Soto (Full Name: Gabriel Soto Díaz) is a Mexican actor and model. As a model, he has successfully won the national male pageant El Modelo Mexico and placed first runner-up in Mister World 1996. Previously, Gabriel played in the Mexican boy band Kairo.

Being an actor, he has featured in several television films and shows including Caer en tentación (2017-2018), Nosotros los guapos (2017), Soltero con hijas (2019-2020), Querida enemiga (2008), and Un refugio para el amor (2012). In 2015 he was awarded both awards of the Best couple with Zuria Vega and Best Kiss with Adriana Louvier. During his free time, Gabriel loves spending time with his family, traveling, and watching.
