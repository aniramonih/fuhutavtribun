Early Life and Musical Beginnings

Gary’s musical journey began in the lively streets of London, where he first picked up the trumpet.

However, his true calling was realized when he switched to the double bass, studying under the esteemed bassist Peter Ind. His early career saw him laying the foundations for what would become a lifelong dedication to jazz.

Founding the Jazz Warriors

In the mid-1980s, Gary Crosby became a founding member of the Jazz Warriors, a pivotal move that marked the beginning of an influential era in British jazz. This ensemble not only showcased his prowess as a bassist but also his capability to collaborate with top-tier talent like Courtney Pine and Steve Williamson.

Educational Contributions

Gary’s passion for education was realized through the establishment of Tomorrow’s Warriors in 1991, a program dedicated to nurturing young jazz talents.

His efforts have been instrumental in shaping the careers of many young musicians, thereby increasing his stature in the jazz community and indirectly contributing to his net worth through educational endeavors.
