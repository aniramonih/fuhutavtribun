His musical talents, combined with an innate ability to make people laugh, led him down the path of comedy, where he found his true calling.

Breaking Into the Scene

The singing comedian with guitar‘s breakthrough came with his appearance on Comedy Central Presents in 2005, a pivotal moment that introduced his unique blend of music and comedy to a wider audience.

But it wasn’t just stand-up that caught the eye of the public; his film Punching the Clown in 2009, which he co-wrote and starred in, showcased his storytelling prowess and won him critical acclaim.
