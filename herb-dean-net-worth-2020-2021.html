Career

Herb Dean debuted as a professional MMA fighter in 2001 in the Gladiator challenge held in California. He went on to win the fight by 1-0.

Later on, Herb started refereeing MMA matches and gained a reputation of a conscientious referee who always took into account the player’s physical fitness and has saved many players from the career-ending injuries.

Till now he has officiated matches for Ultimate fighting championships, King of the Cage, Gladiator Challenge and many more like these.

One of the most memorable fights Herb officiated was that of Sylvia and Mir. During the fight, Dean called for a stop when he saw that Sylvia’s arm had broken. Though Sylvia maintained that nothing of the sort has happened, Herb refused to start the fight again.

Later on, Sylvia acknowledged that his arm was really broken and that Herb had saved him from getting a career-ending injury.

This and many more instances have shown Herb’s fairness and his priority of safety over fun. It has earned him the respect of many with the UTC president calling him the world’s best MMA referee.

Herb has also appeared on shows like the Ultimate Fighter and Elite Extreme.
