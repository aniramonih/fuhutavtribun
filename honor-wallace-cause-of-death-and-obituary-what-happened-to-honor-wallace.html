The serene environment of Southern Methodist University (SMU) was disrupted by the devastating news on September 24, 2023. Honour Wallace, a brilliant young talent and a cherished member of the SMU community, tragically lost her life in a car accident in Grosse Pointe, Michigan.

A Life of Passion and Talent

Hailing from University Liggett School, Honour Wallace wasn’t just another student; she was a beacon of potential. She made her mark at Liggett School as an essential cog in “The Game’s Afoot” editing team. Such was her prowess and commitment that even as a freshman at SMU, her passion for the arts shone brightly. She wore the hat of a Technical Assistant, involving herself in the meticulous art of costume design for various university productions.

SMU, founded in 1911, with its primary campus situated in the heart of University Park, Texas, has always taken pride in its students. It strives to foster an environment where brilliance can blossom. Honour Wallace was a testament to this dedication, showing in her brief stint what true potential looked like.

The Tragic Turn of Events

The fateful day of September 24 will be long remembered with heavy hearts. While the details surrounding the unfortunate incident are still emerging, it is undeniable that a light has dimmed too soon. The SMU community, known for its tight-knit bonds and mutual respect, has been deeply affected. News of Honour’s demise is not just a loss of an excellent student; it’s the loss of future potential, of art, and of unfulfilled dreams.

While Southern Methodist University is yet to release an official statement on this grievous event, the palpable air of melancholy around the campus speaks volumes.

A Community in Mourning

Amidst this sorrow, one can anticipate Honour’s family providing insights into her life and how they’d prefer to commemorate it. Such details would allow her friends, peers, and well-wishers to come together, recollecting the times they shared and celebrating the life that was.

SMU: An Institution of Excellence and Empathy
