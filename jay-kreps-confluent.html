Jay Kreps Bio | Wiki

Jay Kreps is a popular American CEO and co-founder of Confluent, the foundational platform for data in motion built on Apache Kafka. He was previously a Principal Staff Engineer at LinkedIn from June 2007 until September 2014. There, Jay was the technical lead for LinkedIn’s relevance and data systems. This included infrastructure like their storage systems, stream system Apache Kafka, LinkedIn’s search system, recommendation engine and collaborative filtering, and social graph services.

Before that, Jay worked as a Senior Software Engineer, Data Mining at NexTag from April 2006 until July 2007 where they worked on a system for collecting, analyzing, and optimizing data around merchant profitability on a large, high-traffic consumer internet site. He also designed the system used for this from the ground up and was the first employee on the NexTag data mining team when it was created specifically to concentrate on this problem. His career started as a Software Engineer at Movaris in April 2005 which they held until April 2006.
