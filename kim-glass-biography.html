Who is Kim Glass?

Kim Glass is an indoor volleyball player and model of American nationality. Kim Glass plays as an outsider hitter and was named the Region 6 Basketball Tournament Most Valuable Player in 2005.

She was also seen as a contestant in MTV’s reality series The Challenge: Champs vs. Stars in 2017. As of now, she is busy hosting a mobile phone app called Headspace. Here she talks about movement and mindfulness.

Who are the parents of Kim Glass?

Her birthdate is 18 Aug but her birth year is 1984 and she was born in Los Angeles, California, the United States. The name of her mother is Kathy Glass and her father’s name is Sherman Glass.

Further, Kim’s ancestry is All-American, and has two brothers, Darryl and Marcius. She also has two sisters, named Shalana and Shaynce.

As per her academics, Kim Glass completed her school studies at Conestoga Valley High School.

Kim Glass- Profession, Wiki, Career

Kim Glass as a talented volleyball player has participated in many championships. She was in the 2008 Olympic Games and won a silver medal for the team. In the same year, she participated in the U.S. Olympic team exhibition with Brazil and FIVB World Grand Prix.

In 2007, won a bronze medal in FIVB World Cup and came eighth in FIVB World Grand Prix. Then she went to compete in the 2010/11 CEV Champions League and was the runner-up, with Rabita Baku.
