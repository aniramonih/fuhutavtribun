How many celebrities do you think are living a happy and blissful married wife? 

In the case of Scott Pelley, who is CBS News correspondent for 60 Minutes, he is living a happy married life.

Scott Pelley is married to wife Jane Boone, who is a former Channel 5 reporter. The couple, who had met in Dallas for the first time, got married in 1983 on the SMU Campus.

In an interview with Dallasnews.com, Scott said,

“Jane and I lived here for 20 years. We met here, and we were married here on the SMU campus. We had our children here.”

The couple currently has a son Reece Pelley and a daughter Blair Pelley as their children. Both the children are grown up now. The whole family has been all about traveling to different places for a long time.

Travel enthusiast!

In an interview with Chicago Tribune (Published on Dec 2, 2014), Scott Pelly bestowed a piece of advice to the youths. He said:

"Whenever I give commencement addresses at colleges, I tell young people to get a passport. There is no education on earth as good as the education you get from travel. I never tire of going to a place I've never been before. I'm still filled with excitement about getting on a plane, boat or train."

With the father having such exciting vision, the family has traveled to a lot of places. When the interviewer asked him if he likes to travel with his family, he said

“Having our children travel has always been my wife's priority and one Jane (Boone Pelley) has worked very hard to make happen. We've traveled with them since they were itty bitty all over the United States. Then we started going to Europe and Asia and the Middle East. ... We have had the great fortune to be able to take the children to many of the places around the world on many of my "60 Minutes" adventures. I took my son, Reece, to Antarctica when he was about eight years old. It was one of the greatest father-son memories that I have of the two of us. My little boy is now 22 years old and 6-foot-6, but I still remember him as a little guy all wrapped up in his parka in a rubber boat watching the seals and the walruses. We had an amazing time.”

 He has an excellent married life with his wife and children, hasn't he?

The husband and wife recently bought a $2.25 million "two-bedrooms/two bathrooms" condo at 50 West 15th Street in the Oculus Condominium in New York. They are looking forward to making it their second home. They currently live in Connecticut in their 8,000-square-foot home. 

Scott Pelley’s Short Bio:

Scott Pelley was born on July 28, 1957, (age 59), in Texas, U.S. Scott attended the Texas Tech University and majored in Journalism there. He had started to work as a reporter even before getting his degree.

He joined the CBS Network in 1989. He is known for his award-winning stories for "60 Minutes"(since 2003) and "60 Minutes II."

He has also been recognized because of his war reports for CBS Evening News with Scott Pelley (since 2012). He was repeatedly awarded Emmy Awards for his reportings. He has also been the host and anchor of the show CBS Evening News with Scott Pelley since 2011.  He currently has a net worth of $16 million dollars with a salary of $7 million dollars.
