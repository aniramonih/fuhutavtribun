Krushna Abhishek Overview and Wiki

Krushna Abhishek started his journey in the bustling city of Mumbai, where he was born to Atmaprakash Sharma and Padma Sharma. Raised in a family with a cinematic lineage, including his uncle, the legendary actor Govinda, Krushna was always close to the glitter of Bollywood. His sister, Aarti Singh, is also a noted television actress.

Stepping into the Limelight

He embarked on his acting career with the film Yeh Kaisi Mohabbat Hai in 2002, marking the beginning of a journey filled with laughter and applause. However, it was his participation in Comedy Circus that catapulted him to fame, showcasing his innate talent for humor.

Dancing His Way to Fame

Not just a comedian, Krushna also shone brightly as a dancer. His participation in Nach Baliye 3 and Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 4 was a testament to his versatility, drawing inspiration from his dance icon and uncle, Govinda.

The Rise as a Television Host

Krushna’s charisma and quick wit led him to host several popular TV shows, including The Kapil Sharma Show, where he became a household name. His role in the show not only brought him immense popularity but also substantial financial gains, with a per-episode salary of Rs. 12 Lakhs.
