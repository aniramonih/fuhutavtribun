Len Goodman children: Meet his son, James William Goodman

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Len Goodman is a retired professional ballroom dancer and choreographer from the United Kingdom.

He is best known for his role as a judge on the popular television show “Strictly Come Dancing,” the UK version of “Dancing with the Stars,” as well as its American counterpart.

Goodman was born on April 25, 1944, in Bethnal Green, London. He began dancing at a young age and turned professional in 1962.

He went on to have a successful career as a competitive dancer, winning numerous championships in the ballroom dance world.

In addition to his work on “Strictly Come Dancing” and “Dancing with the Stars,” Goodman has also worked as a choreographer for a number of television shows and films, including “The Muppets” and “Dirty Dancing: Havana Nights.”

Goodman announced his retirement from “Strictly Come Dancing” in 2016 but continued to appear on the show occasionally as a guest judge. He currently lives in England with his wife, Sue, and has four grandchildren.
