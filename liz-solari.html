The Argentine actress Liz Solari is married to businessman Walter Fara.

Liz Solari is an Argentine entertainment personality. Solari is a model and an arising actress, who has starred in several theatrical shows, movies, and TV shows like The Last Man, Evil Woman, and Champs 12, among others. 

The beautiful actress was born as Maria Isabel Solari Poggio on June 18, 1983, in Barranquilla, Colombia. She is the only daughter of former football player Eduardo Solari and his wife. Liz has three brothers Esteban, David, and Santiago Solari. All of them are footballers.

Liz earned her master's degree in acting from the Central School of Speech and Drama located in Belsize Park, London, UK. She also enrolled at the Guildford School of Acting.

Happily Married Woman

The 37-years-old actress Liz is in a marital relationship with Walter Fara. Liz's husband (marido) Walter is an entrepreneur by profession. Walter is the senior manager and the part of the global business development team at TIDAL. Fara graduated from the University of the Arts London.

Liz and Walter first met one another back in 2011. After the first encounter, they shared a good bond and began their romantic affair. But they temporarily separated for a few years. Later, the pair rekindled their romantic relationship and tied the wedding knot on June 18, 2018.

The longtime couple shared the wedding vows in Sardinia, Italy. Solari shared the news of her marriage with Walter via her Instagram post. 

Second Marriage In Mexico

After tieing the wedding knot for the first time in June, Liz & Walter married for the second time in November 2018. This time the couple chooses Sian Ka'an, Mexico, to renew their vows.

The intimate outdoor wedding ceremony was held in front of their dearest ones at Ka'an Tulum, restaurant and beach club. On the big day, the bride Liz wore a beautiful V-shaped backless wedding gown.

Similarly, the groom Walter dashes the wedding look wearing a blue tuxedo. As of now, they are sharing a good bond as a perfect married pair.

Former Boyfriend Passed Away

Before sharing an intimate life with the businessman Walter, a model, actress, Liz was previously linked with several men. Solari's then-boyfriend (novio) actor Leonardo Verhagen died (fallecido) at the age of 28 back in January 2010.

Leonardo passed away because of a heart attack. About the tragic incident, Liz revealed,

"We went home, opened a bottle of wine, played some music, went upstairs and made love. Immediately after we finished, he began to have convulsions and fell on the floor. It was desperate to see him die in my arms."

A year after Verhagen's demise, Liz met Walter and start a new chapter of her romantic life. As of 2020, she is living merrily with him. 

For the information, besides Leonardo, Liz was linked with Diego Balut, Martin Ricci, Mariano Martinez, Marcelo Tinelli, Pancho Dotto, and Rodrigo Guirao Diaz.
