Maria Shriver Married Life With Ex-husband Arnold Schwarzenegger

Maria Shriver and the former governor of California, Arnold Schwarzenegger, had been married for 25 years after they decided to separate in 2011. The split was due to Arnold’s affair with their housekeeper that resulted in the birth of their child, Joseph Baena who was born in the same year as their youngest son, Christopher.

The couple had released a joint statement in 2011 announcing their split and said, “This has been a time of great personal and professional transition for each of us. After a great deal of thought, reflection, discussion, and prayer, we came to this decision together. At this time, we are living apart while we work on the future of our relationship. We are continuing to parent our four children together. They are the light and the center of both of our lives. We consider this a private matter and neither we nor any of our friends or family will have further comment.”

The couple first met at a charity tennis tournament in 1977 and got married after 9 years of dating in 1986. They tied the knot at St. Francis Xavier Church in Hyannis, Massachusetts.

Maria Shriver seems to be dating political analyst, Matthew Dowd since 2014. They first met when he was helping her ex-husband, Arnold with his election campaign. However, they started getting romantic with each other after her split with her husband. At the time of her split, Dowd was a major support system for Maria and they ultimately are together till today.

Maria Shriver had shared her daughter Katherine’s wedding experiences in Today with Hoda and Jenna and described her daughter’s wedding day as an emotional rollercoaster day for her saying, “As a mother, you go through many emotions, right? So you’re trying to be both excited, you get a new member of your family, but you’re kind of losing your child as they were. So you’re kind of excited and everybody’s like, ‘Well, isn’t this great? Net Worth Of Maria Shriver

Maria Shriver’s net worth is estimated to be around $200 million according to Celebrity Net Worth. She is an American journalist, author, and former first lady of California and best known as an ex-wife of Arnold Schwarzenegger.

Apart from her relation to her ex-husband, Maria Shriver is an award-winning American television journalist and author who has secured two Emmy awards and a Peabody Award. She is the founder of Shriver Media that is known for delivering content on an array of topics that include social change and women’s empowerment. She is also a seven-time New York Times best-selling author.

Maria Shriver graduated from Georgetown University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in American studies. She is now an anchor for NBC news. She is also the niece of former US President John F. Kennedy.

She started her career as an anchor on The CBS Morning News with Forrest Sawyer on Philadelphia's KYW-TV. She has also worked as a producer in several media projects such as her TV documentary series titled, The Alzheimer’s Project which won two Emmy Awards.
