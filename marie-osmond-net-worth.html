Marie Osmond Net Worth: A Tale of Talent, Triumph, and Two Marriages

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Marie Osmond net worth is about $20 million. She is primarily recognized for her association with the Osmonds, her family’s music group.

This article will delve into her biography, age, career, and the path she took to amass her wealth.

Early Life

Olive Marie Osmond, born on October 13, 1959, in Ogden, Utah, grew up with eight siblings as a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

She witnessed her brothers building showbiz careers from a young age but typically did not officially perform with the Osmonds, despite making occasional appearances alongside them on television.

What is Marie Osmond Net Worth and Salary?

The American actress and singer Marie Osmond, with a net worth of $20 million, is primarily recognized for her association with her family’s music ensemble, the Osmonds.

While Marie was not a formal member of the group, she established her own reputation as a solo Country artist.

Furthermore, she has hosted and participated as a panelist in numerous prestigious television programs, such as ABC’s “The Talk.”

Career

Marie Osmond has entertained millions worldwide for over six decades through various mediums. She has reached her fans through records, television, dancing, acting, writing, endorsing products, entrepreneurship, and public speaking.

She has maintained her global recognition and success throughout the years.

As the only girl among nine siblings in an iconic entertainment family, Marie embarked on her career at the age of 3.

She was playfully introduced as the youngest Osmond “brother” on the Andy Williams Show, and from there, she started performing with her brothers in the United States, Europe, and Asia.

Marie Osmond Husband, Why She Remarried Her Ex-Husband Stephen Craig

Marie fell in love with Stephen Craig, her husband whom she married twice. She first met the former pro-basketball player when she was a teenager.

At the age of 22, The Talk co-host married Stephen for the first time on June 26, 1982, in front of 4,000 guests.

However, in March 2007, Marie and her second husband, Brian Blosil, announced their divorce. Two years later, she remarried Craig after dating for a while.

Marie Osmond Net Worth, Family and Her Eight Children

Marie Osmond’s personal life mirrors the busyness of her professional career. Besides her multifaceted entertainment career, the Donny & Marie star is a mother of eight.

She has a son, Stephen Jr., with her husband, Stephen Craig Sr. The couple divorced in 1989 but remarried in 2011.

Additionally, she and her ex-husband, Brian Blosil, have seven children together: Rachael, Jessica, Michael, Brandon, Brianna, Matthew, and Abigail.

Tragically, her son Michael passed away in 2010 at the age of 18.
