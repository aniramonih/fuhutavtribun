So exciting! Marie Osmond‘s daughter Jessica and her wife Sara have tied the knot!

The 59-year-old took to Instagram to share the exciting news with all of her fans. “Congratulations to my darling daughter Jessica and her new wife Sara! I’m so glad I got to fly in for the day to be apart of such a happy occasion!” the legendary performer captioned alongside photos of the pair making it official. “I could not love the two of you more! Welcome to our family!

Take a look at the full post below!

Marie’s fans were of course all about the news, as they took to the comments section to show their glee. “Absolutely beautiful. Thanks for being an amazing mama bear,” one person said. Another added, “I am beyond thrilled that you accept [Jessica] for who she is. Thank you for believing that love is love. From one LGBT ally to another, much respect and peace.”

Jessica, 31, isn’t the only child that the singer has — she has seven more kids from two relationships. Marie welcomed her first child, son Stephen, 36, with hubby Steve Craig in 1983. After the pair divorced, the “A Little Bit Country, a Little Bit Rock ‘n’ Roll” songstress tied the knot with Brian Blosil in 1986.

Before splitting in 2007, Marie and Brian had daughter Rachael, 29 and son Matthew, 19, and adopted five kids including Jessica — Michael, Brandon, 22, Brianna, 21, and Abigail, 1. Unfortunately, the star’s son Michael passed away at age 18 in February 2010.

Donny Osmond‘s sister just recently celebrated another wedding in her family — in December 2018, her daughter Brianna married her fiancé. “#Congratulations to our beautiful daughter Brianna and her wonderful husband David Schwep. It was an amazing and glorious day!!! We love you both so much and eternally,” Marie captioned a picture of the two lovebirds. She added, “#MrAndMrsSchwep #FavoritePhoto #Eternity #SweptOffHerFeet #SchwepicDay #FamiliesAreForever.”

We wish Jessica, Sara and everyone in the Osmond family nothing but the best!
