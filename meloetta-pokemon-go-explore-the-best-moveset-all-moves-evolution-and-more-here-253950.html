Meloetta In Pokemon Go

Meloetta is a mythical Normal and Psychic type Pokemon in Pokemon GO with a maximum CP of 4490, 250 attack, 225 defence, and 225 stamina. It was discovered in the Unova region at first (Gen 5). Meloetta is weak to moves of the Bug and Dark types. Partly Cloudy and Windy conditions favour Meloetta. Quick Attack and Hyper Beam are Meloetta's greatest moves (17.70 DPS)
