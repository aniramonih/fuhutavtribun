Meredith Actress

Meredith made her screen debut in 2008 in the CBS daytime soap opera As the World Turns as Liberty Ciccone, the illegitimate daughter of Brad Snyder and Janet Ciccone. In 2009, she was chosen for a Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Younger Actress in a Drama Series. She appeared in the FX short-lived 2011 drama series Lights Out.

In 2012, she started appearing as Amy Jordan in the TBS comedy series Men at Work. The series was canceled in 2014 after three seasons. In 2016, Meredith came back to network television in TBS dark comedy Search Party.
