Meet Michael Lewis Wife, Tabitha Soren, A Fine Art Photographer

Tabitha Soren is not just the wife of Michael Lewis; she is a talented American fine art photographer and a former reporter.

She has worked at MTV News, ABC News, and NBC News.

The Lewis-Soren couple’s enduring love story began when they tied the knot in October 1997.

Their relationship, characterized by mutual support and creativity, is admired within and beyond their industry.

The married duo reportedly resides in Berkeley, California, where they pursue their creative passions.

Soren’s photography projects have graced numerous publications and museums, while Lewis’s literary works have inspired successful films and podcasts.

Together, they embody a dynamic, inspiring partnership that has weathered challenges and joys.

Moreover, Tabitha Soren is not Michael Lewis’s first wife. Before his marriage to Tabitha, Lewis had been married twice.

The prominent writer’s first marriage was to Diane de Cordova Lewis in 1985, and his second to former CNBC correspondent Kate Bohner.

Despite these earlier unions, his enduring marriage to Tabitha Soren has captured the public’s imagination.

Michael Lewis Daughter And Family: Proud Parents of Three Children

Beyond their successful careers, Michael Lewis and Tabitha Soren are devoted parents to three children: Quinn, Dixie, and Walker.

Dixie, a rising sophomore and talented softball player at Pomona College, left a void that her parents and siblings will forever feel.

Lewis expressed how his family has coped with the tragedy they have faced.
