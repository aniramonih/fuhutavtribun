Milla Jovovich Net Worth: How Rich is the American Actress?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Milla Jovovich is an American actress and model. She’s popular for acting tough roles in action and science fiction films, especially in the Resident Evil series. She is also a singer, fashion designer, and a successful model.

Recently, her net worth has become a topic of interest among fans, as they want to know how much she has earned. In this post, you will find all the information related to her earnings.

What is Milla Jovovich’s Net Worth?

As of 2023, Milla Jovovich’s net worth was estimated to be around $80 million. She made a lot of money from acting in movies like “Resident Evil,” modeling for brands, doing fashion endeavors, and working on music projects.

She’s good at handling different ways to earn money and staying famous in showbiz, showing she’s smart and talented in business.

How Did Milla Jovovich Became Famous?

While most kids were in high school, Milla went to acting schools. At just 13, she starred in her first movie, Two Moon Junction. Even though she was still a young teen, she got big roles in films like Return to the Blue Lagoon and Dazed and Confused after being in TV shows.

Her breakthrough happened in 1997 with The Fifth Element, a sci-fi movie that made over $263 million. She continued with roles in He Got Game, The Messenger: The Story of Joan of Arc, and Zoolander.

Milla got a record deal in 1988. She released her first official album, “The Divine Comedy,” in 1994. This album had songs she wrote herself, acoustic guitar tunes, and a mix of pop and traditional Ukrainian folk songs. Critics liked the album.

In 1999, she started an experimental band called Plastic Has Memory. Besides making music for her movies, Milla worked with artists like The Crystal Method and Deepak Chopra.

In 2002, Milla’s career soared when she starred in Resident Evil, a film based on a popular video game. It earned over $100 million, and she went on to be in six Resident Evil movies, making the franchise earn a whopping $1.2 billion. Despite critics not liking them, these movies are the highest-grossing ones based on a video game.

Apart from Resident Evil, Milla is also famous for being in movies like The Fourth Kind, The Three Musketeers, Zoolander 2, Future World, Paradise Hills, and the 2019 Hellboy reboot.

How Did Milla Jovovich Became a Highest Paid Model?

Jovovich made a lot of money as a model. At times, she was the highest-paid model in the world, like in 2004, when she earned over $10.5 million. She started her career as a teen and appeared on magazine covers like Cosmopolitan and Vogue.

She did big projects with famous brands such as Banana Republic, Christian Dior, Versace, and Giorgio Armani. Even though modeling wasn’t her main focus, Jovovich earned respect in the industry.

Celebrities like Miuccia Prada and Gianni Versace admired her. She also worked with another model, Carmen Hawk, to create a successful fashion line, but they stopped the business in 2008.
