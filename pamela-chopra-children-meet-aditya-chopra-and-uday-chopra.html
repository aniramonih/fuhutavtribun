Pamela Chopra children: Meet Aditya Chopra and Uday Chopra

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Pamela Chopra was an Indian playback singer. She was the wife of veteran Bollywood film director Yash Chopra and was also a film writer and producer in her own right.

Chopra was born Pamela Singh, the child of Indian Army commander Mohinder Singh. She has two younger brothers and was the middle kid out of three.

Chopra attended many army schools because her father was stationed in several rural areas of India. She was related to Simi Garewal, a famous actress. Garewal’s mother Darshi Garewal and Chopra’s father Mohinder Singh were related.

In 1970, Pamela wed the director Yash Chopra. Their relatives organized the marriage in the conventional Indian way.

The mother of filmmaker Romesh Sharma, who produced the movie Hum, was friends with both families. Sharma got in touch with BR Chopra’s wife and advised that Pamela Singh would make ‘the ideal bride’ for BR’s younger brother Yash Chopra.

Chopra had experience in a variety of film-related industries. She had performed several cinema songs, all of which were for her husband’s movies, such as Mujhse Dosti Karoge! (2002) and Kabhie Kabhie (1976).

In addition, her name was listed as a “producer” on the credits of some of her husband’s films. She did, however, independently produce the 1993 movie Aaina. Together with her husband Yash Chopra, her son Aditya Chopra, and seasoned screenwriter Tanuja Chandra, Pamela co-wrote the script for her husband’s 1997 film Dil To Pagal Hai.

What happened to Pamela Chopra?

Pamela Chopra died at Lilavati Hospital in Mumbai at age 74 on 20 April 2023.

Who are Pamela Chopra children?

Pamela Chopra has two children with her husband Yash Chopra whose names are Chopra and Uday Chopra. Aditya was born in 1971 and Uday was born in 1973. Aditya is a film producer and director. He is married to actress Rani Mukerji. Uday is an actor and film producer.

Who is Aditya Chopra?

Aditya Chopra is a studio executive, film distributor, producer, writer, and director from India is known as. He currently serves as the Chairman of India’s multi-national film, media, and entertainment giant Yash Raj Films (YRF). He is widely considered as one of the most prominent executives in Indian cinema industry.

Who is Uday Chopra?

Uday Raj Chopra is an Indian actor and producer. In the 2000 musical romantic drama Mohabbatein, he made his acting debut. He later appeared in a number of other movies, such as Mere Yaar Ki Shaadi Hai (2002), Dhoom (2004), Dhoom 2 (2006), and Dhoom 3 (2013).
