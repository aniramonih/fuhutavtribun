Siena Agudong is an American actress, social media personality, and YouTuber who is known for having been cast in a wide range of characters in several shows and movies such as Lulu Parker in Killer Women, Sophia Miller in Star Falls, Brittany in Sydney to the Max, Reagan Wills in Alex & Me, and Nick in No Good Nick. She began her acting pursuits at a very young age and has often said that she has been greatly inspired by her older sister, Sydney. Over the years, Siena Agudong has appeared in a couple more shows and movies like Teachers, Second Chances, and Teens Wanna Know and has also been nominated for several awards such as Young Artist Award and Young Entertainer Award while she won the Young Entertainer Award in 2017. In September 2013, she joined YouTube with her eponymous channel on which she has uploaded a number of entertaining videos and she has also released a few webisodes titled Mindy’s Moment’s and Madame Rosa. Siena Agudong is also very close to her family and loves playing soccer. Moreover, she has also garnered a huge social media fan base with more than 800k followers on Instagram, more than 20k subscribers on YouTube, and more than 10k followers on Twitter.
