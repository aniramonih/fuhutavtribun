The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Friday, June 17 Li Tricks Sheila Vows No Cops &amp; Support

August 2024 · 4 minute read

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers for Friday, June 17, tease that Li Finnegan (Naomi Matsuda) will trick Sheila Carter (Kimberlin Brown) with fake acceptance.

Li will vow no cops and support Sheila’s love for John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan), so she’ll be doing whatever she has to do for her son’s sake.

Before we dive in, there’s a quick programming note to pass along. B&B was preempted on Thursday, June 16, so that pushes Thursday’s episode to Friday, June 17.

Fortunately, we’ve got plenty of new details on what’s happening, so here’s what fans can look forward to.

At Li’s hideout, Sheila will realize she needs Li’s medical expertise. Since Sheila just threw Li to the floor, she’ll push her to get up and help Finn.

Li is obviously in a tricky situation, so her best strategy is to play along and pretend to accept Sheila.

That’s exactly what Li will do, so she’ll tell Sheila she’s been listening and is starting to believe she truly loves their son.

It’ll warm Sheila’s cold heart to hear Li think of Finn in that way – as the son who belongs to both of them. Sheila will end up embracing Li and thanking her for saving Finn’s life.

All that’ll matter to Li now is Finn, so she’ll act like they have to come together. Li will mention running low on supplies and will assure Sheila that she’s not going to get the police involved.

Since Li faked Finn’s death, she’ll argue that she would get in trouble herself and would at the very least lose her medical license.

Of course, there’s no way Li would legitimately accept Sheila’s love for Finn or consider her any kind of ally. Li is setting Sheila up for a fall here and will face Sheila’s wrath as a result during the week of June 20-24.

In the meantime, Finn will drift in and out of consciousness, but Li will stick to her plan and tell him that BOTH of his mothers are there with him.

Sheila will be lulled into a false sense of security as Li lays it on thick.

Next, Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) and Taylor Hayes (Krista Allen) will continue to fret over Sheila’s escape, so Ridge will wind up ranting at Deputy Chief Bradley Baker (Dan Martin) over the phone.

After Ridge pushes Baker to find Sheila before someone else gets hurt, Taylor will acknowledge the threat Sheila still poses.

There’ll be concerns about Sheila’s next move and who she could go after now that she’s on the loose.

Over with Hope Spencer (Annika Noelle), she’ll confront Eric Forrester (John McCook) about his affair and will reveal what she saw at the beach club.

Eric will assure Hope that he’s not a bad person, but he’ll struggle to explain why he’s doing something he knows is wrong.

Although Eric forgave Quinn Forrester (Rena Sofer) for her betrayal, he’ll talk about what Donna Logan (Jennifer Gareis) brings to his life as well.

Hope will get why Eric is torn, but she’ll think he needs to get honest about what’s in his heart and figure out which woman he truly wants.

At Forrester Creations, Quinn will speak to Carter Walton (Lawrence Saint-Victor) about how things went with Bridget Forrester (Ashley Jones) and will feel a bit guilty for keeping info from Eric regarding the smart ring.

Quinn will also show off some new gemstones, so that’ll lead to a close moment where Carter touches her hand and puts a ring on her finger.

As old feelings rush back on both sides, Carter will get flustered and once again insist he has to move on.

When Carter starts to rush right out the door, Quinn will urge him to stop.

The connection between Carter and Quinn will remain intense, but he’s on the verge of moving full-speed ahead with Paris through an imminent engagement!

As other B&B news becomes available, we’ll deliver more updates. The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say Quinn will have to watch Carter make some extreme moves soon, so stay tuned.
