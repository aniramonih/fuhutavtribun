Full Story

Alexandra “Xandra” Pohl is a 22-year-old content creator who has recently become popular on TikTok with almost 1 million followers. She credits her success to the community of influencers who support each other and provide advice when needed. Although new to the influencer world, Xandra is enjoying every bit of her experience and is grateful for the opportunities that come her way.

Xandra’s first “Get Ready With Me” TikTok video was a hit with over 2 million views, prompting her to pursue a career as an influencer. She acknowledges that the lavish brand trips, which are a big feature of the lifestyle, have made headlines, but Xandra appreciates them for the opportunity to meet other influencers who share her passion for makeup, skincare, and fashion. She is pleased that brands are interested in working with her and grateful for the experiences she has had on those trips.

One person who has been instrumental in Xandra’s journey as an influencer is her longtime friend and fellow TikTok star, Alix Earle. They have been best friends since before freshman year started, and she could not imagine the journey without her. Xandra appreciates the support and guidance that Alix provides, and both have been on several brand trips together, including to Dubai.

In conclusion, Xandra is a rising star in the influencer world, and she is grateful for the community of influencers who support each other. Her experiences on brand trips have been unforgettable, and she considers it a privilege to be invited by the brands. Xandra and Alix demonstrate the power of friendship, and their journey together is proof that having someone to rely on makes all the difference.
