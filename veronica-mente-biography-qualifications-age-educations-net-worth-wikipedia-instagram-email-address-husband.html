Career

Veronica Mente started nurturing her political ambition as far back as when she was in school. Though she could not hold any popular position, she was an active member of campus politics. She practically began her political career after school by becoming a member of the security for the former Mayor of Cape Town.

In 2013 when EFF was founded by Julius Malema and Kenny Kunene, she was part of the very first people to join the party. In 2014, she was elected to serve in Parliament. She was permitted to continue another term in office as MP. Ms. after spending her first 5 years full term.

Veronica Mente is a Standing Committee on Public Accounts member and an Alternate Member of the Standing Committee on the Auditor General. In September 2019, Veronica Mente emerged as a member of the Disciplinary Committee.

In December of the same year (2019), she was announced as the EFF national Chairperson after Advocate Dali Mpofu completed his term in office.
