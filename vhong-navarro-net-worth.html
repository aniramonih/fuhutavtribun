FAQs about Vhong Navarro

Who is Vhong Navarro?

Born Ferdinand Hipolito Navarro on January 4, 1977, he is a multifaceted Filipino celebrity known for his work as a comedian, actor, dancer, recording artist, and television host.

How did he start his career?

He began his entertainment career in the early 1990s as a member of the dance group Streetboys, which paved the way for his ventures into acting and hosting.

What are some of his notable works?

He has gained recognition for his comedic roles in movies such as Agent X44, Mr. Suave, and the Spider-Man parody film Gagamboy. Additionally, he is a regular host on ABS-CBN’s noontime variety show It’s Showtime.

Has he received any awards for his work?

Yes, he has been honored with several awards throughout his career, including the Gawad Urian Award for Best Actor in 2014 for his role in The Amazing Praybeyt Benjamin and was named Best Male TV Host by the PMPC Star Awards for Television in 2015.

What legal issues has he faced?

In 2022, Navarro surrendered to the National Bureau of Investigation after trial courts in Taguig issued him separate warrants for rape and acts of lasciviousness committed against Deniece Cornejo in 2014.

However, the Philippine Supreme Court later dismissed these cases.

When was he released from jail?

After being incarcerated for 79 days on charges filed by model Deniece Cornejo, Navarro was released from jail on December 7, 2022, following his payment of P1 million for provisional release.

What is Vhong Navarro’s personality like?

He is known for his energetic and charismatic presence, often entertaining audiences through dance and comedy. He has a strong sense of humor and enjoys interacting with his audiences, creating a strong connection with them.

What is his marital status?

He has been married twice. His first marriage was to Bianca Lapus, which ended in divorce. He is currently married to Tanya Bautista, a writer for ABS-CBN, with whom he tied the knot on November 28, 2019, in Japan.

How did he recover from a brutal attack?

In January 2014, he was attacked by six men, necessitating surgery. Despite the severity of the assault, he was able to recover and continue his career in entertainment.
