Vilailuck Teigen Bio, Family, Facts of Chrissy Teigens Mother

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Vilailuck Teigen (born December 12, 1961) is a Thai-born American Instagram Star, Famous Personality, and Celebrity Parent from Beverly Hills, California. She is best known as Chrissy Teigen’s mom (American model, television personality and author).

The model initially gained success after making debut in the annual Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue in 2010. Furthermore, Teigen was first discovered by a photographer when she used to work in a surf shop.

Later, she signed her first deal with IMG Models. Chrissy even served as a recurring guest host and contributor on E!, TMZ, MTV, FUSE/MSG, and Extra. Furthermore, Vilailuck’s kid starred on America’s Next Top Model and Watch What Happens Live.

As an author, Chrissy Teigen made debut after releasing the book “Cravings: Recipes for All of the Food You Want to Eat” on February 23, 2016.

Personal Life, Spouse & Children

The 57-years-old Vilailuck Teigen tied the knot with her husband “Ron Teigen Sr.” many years ago. Ron worked as an electrician and is of Norwegian & German descent. The couple welcomed two kids together.

On November 30, 1985, Vilailuck gave birth to their first daughter named Christine Diane Teigen. Later, she and her spouse blessed with their second girl Tina Teigen. In fact, they are also the grandparents of Chrissy’s children.

On September 14, 2013, the model married her longtime boyfriend, John Legend in Como, Italy. He is an American singer, songwriter, producer, actor, and philanthropist. The pair has a daughter “Luna Stephens” (b. April 14, 2016) and a son “Miles Stephens” (b. May 16, 2018).

The model’s father, Ron, filed a divorce from Vilailuck on November 05, 2019 in Los Angeles, California. However, the cause of their separation is yet not disclosed. Last year, in 2018, Chrissy’s mother began to live with her and John.
