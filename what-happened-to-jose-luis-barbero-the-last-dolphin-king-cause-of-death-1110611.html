Who Is Jose Luis Barbero?

Jose Luis Barbero was a Spanish Dolphin trainer. He had a great passion for working in aquariums to train Dolphins. He worked as a Dolphin Trainer for more than three decades in various aquariums and water parks. He is one of the most famous dolphin trainers in the world. He was a vice president at Georgia Aquarium located in Atlanta, United States. Before being promoted to vice president, he worked in Marineland Mallorca in Spain.

What Happened To Jose Luis Barbero?

An animal activist group SOSdelfines released a 99-second video about Jose Luis Barbero. In that video, Jose Luis Barbero kicked and hit the dolphins he was training. This news outbursted in social media. The video was blurred with hidden camera footage. Jose Luis Barbero refused that he was not the person in that video and it was revenge to wash out his professional reputation by his former employees. FBI agents investigated this case. A few days later, Jose Luis Barbero's body was found dead in Spain. Still, nothing has been discovered whether Jose Luis Barbero was innocent or not.

Jose Luis Barbero Cause Of Death

Jose Luis Barbero was very upset after his video released about torturing dolphins. He had to face more hatred on Social media and also in his surroundings. He received many death threats after the video's release. On 3 March 2015, He went missing, and police searched for him, but they couldn't reach him. On 7 March 2015, his body was found dead in his vehicle in at the Sont Sant Joan airport. He was 59 years old when he died. His cause of death is Suicide.
