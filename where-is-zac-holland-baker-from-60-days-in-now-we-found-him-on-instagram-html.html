Where is Zac Holland Baker from 60 Days In now? We found him on Instagram!

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Ever since the arrival of the first season of ’60 Days In’ on Netflix, the show, which originally aired on A&E three years ago, has garnered a brand new fanbase. This captivating docuseries sends ordinary civilians undercover into prisons, where they pose as inmates to uncover instances of police corruption and address issues within the correctional system.

The initial two seasons of the show were set in Clark County Jail in Jeffersonville, Indiana, and one of the standout cast members from the first season was Zac. So, who exactly is Zac, and where has life taken him since his time on the show?

Meet Zac, a Former Marine with a Purpose:

Zac, whose full name is Zachary Baker, was a key participant in season 1 of ’60 Days In.’ Originally hailing from Knoxville, Tennessee, he adopted the alias ‘Zac Holland’ during his time on the show to safeguard his true identity.

Notably, Zac was a former US Marine who remained in reserve status when he embarked on this daring undercover mission. His motivation for participating in the experiment was twofold: he sought to gain insight into life on the other side of the law, and he aspired to pursue a career as a Drug Enforcement Agency (DEA) agent.

During season 1, Zac emerged as one of the most beloved participants, capturing the hearts of viewers with his compelling journey.

Zac’s Mission on ’60 Days In’:

Beyond his curiosity about the criminal justice system, Zac had a deeply personal motive for participating in ’60 Days In.’ He aimed to spend 60 days in jail to better understand and empathize with the struggles his wife, Ashleigh, had endured.

Ashleigh had confronted her own battles with addiction and alcohol abuse, even serving time in jail herself. Interestingly, Ashleigh later joined the cast for season 2 of ’60 Days In.’

While their time on the show exposed certain marital challenges, catching up with Zac and Ashleigh in the years that followed reveals a happier and more content couple.

Tracking Zac’s Whereabouts:

To get a glimpse into Zac’s life since his ’60 Days In’ experience, the producers of the show conducted a ‘where are they now?’ special, which aired six months after filming had concluded. At that time, Zac was encountering challenges in his pursuit of a career in law enforcement.

While specific details about Zac’s activities over the past three years remain somewhat elusive, we managed to locate him on Instagram under the handle @unashamedlyscared. Although Zac doesn’t frequently update his profile with personal news, his Instagram features an array of prank videos where he enjoys playfully scaring his wife, Ashleigh.

Notably, Ashleigh also maintains an active presence on Instagram, where she shares numerous updates about their life together. The couple’s bond appears as strong as it was during their time on the show, and they now cherish the joys of parenthood with their two young sons, Gavin and Ryker.

For those interested in following Ashleigh’s journey, her Instagram handle is @unashamed1, boasting nearly 20,000 followers who eagerly keep up with her updates.

In the world of ’60 Days In,’ Zac may have ventured behind bars as part of a unique mission, but today, he enjoys the simple pleasures of family life, as evidenced by the heartwarming content he and Ashleigh share on their Instagram accounts.
